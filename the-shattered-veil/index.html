The History

The Shattering

The Vision

Archmage-Empress Kalithene spent fifty years constructing seventeen massive pylons across three continents. Each would anchor a permanent connection to a different dimension: realms of fire, celestial heavens, demonic depths, elemental chaos. She planned to filter beneficial energies while blocking dangers. Divine guidance without judgment. Elemental power without destruction.

Six Hours of Paradise

On the Summer Solstice of Year 667, the ritual began. For six glorious hours, it worked perfectly. Angels walked among humans. Magical metals formed freely. Paradise bloomed across three continents. Kalithene's consciousness held seventeen contradictory realities in perfect balance.

The Shattering

Then came the handoff. Transferring control to the pylons themselves. Her mind couldn't hold the paradox. She shattered. The filters failed. Every plane poured through uncontrolled. Cities drowned. Forests ignited. Demons invaded. Sixty percent of humanity died in the first week.

A Thousand Years Later

The world isn't post-apocalyptic anymore. It's weird but functional. The pylons still stand, indestructible and impossible to shut off. Seventeen fragment zones spread across three continents, each transformed by its connected dimension. Humanity adapted. Civilization rebuilt. The fragments became home.

The Approach

What Makes This Different

Alignment

Geographic, Not Philosophical

Alignment is geographic, not philosophical. Stay in Mount Celestia's shadow long enough and you become lawful good whether you wanted to or not. The zones don't care about your backstory.

Adventurers

Professional Specialists

No prophecies. No chosen ones. Characters are skilled professionals doing dangerous work because someone has to. The Veilwalkers Guild trains, equips, deploys, and expects results.

Societies

Functional Under Influence

After a thousand years, people figured it out. Demon-ruled valleys have functioning economies. Celestial zones have crime (sort of). Every society adapted to its planar reality.

Content

Ready to Run

Complete settlements with NPCs you can voice in seconds. Session-by-session adventure guides. Quick reference tables. Everything built for Game Masters who prep in stolen moments, not dedicated hours.
