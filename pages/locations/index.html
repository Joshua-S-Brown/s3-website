Solverde

Beach settlement · Azure Cove · Cerulean Sea

A beach town built at animal scale from salvaged human materials. Whitewashed walls, terracotta roofs, sea-glass windows. The name comes from old human resort signage. The humans meant it as marketing. The animals made it true.
