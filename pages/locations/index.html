Locations

The places where furmanity lives

Every place has a history longer than the animals who live there. The buildings were human once. The streets were human once. What furmanity does with what was left behind says more about who they are than any speech or charter.

Settlements

Solverde

Beach settlement · Azure Cove · Cerulean Sea

A beach town built at animal scale from salvaged human materials. Whitewashed walls, terracotta roofs, sea-glass windows. The name comes from old human resort signage. The humans meant it as marketing. The animals made it true.

Being charted

The Keepers record places as furmanity settles them. The Lighthouse, the Old Dockyards, and Botan's Garden are next in the record.
