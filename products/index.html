Less Prep. More Play.

Second Self Studios creates premium D&D 5e content for people who love running games but have limited time to prepare. Every product is designed to minimize prep while maximizing table impact.

The Problem We Solve

You have a career, a family, responsibilities. You get maybe an hour or two to prep before game night. Most published content assumes you have unlimited time to read dense lore, cross-reference stat blocks, and adapt generic adventures to your table.

We build differently. Our content is organized for how DMs actually prep: quick reference tables, clear session structures, NPCs with personalities you can voice in seconds, and adventures that tell you exactly what you need to know and nothing more.

Design Philosophy

The Active Content Test

Every element must answer one question: "Does this create an adventure hook?" If a piece of lore, an NPC detail, or a location description doesn't drive play, it doesn't belong. No passive worldbuilding. No encyclopedia entries. Everything exists to give you something to use at the table.

  • Modular by Design

    Every zone, faction, and adventure works standalone or as part of a larger campaign. Use what you need, ignore what you don't. No required reading to run a session.

  • Preparation Rewarded

    When players research enemies, scout locations, or gather intel, they get real mechanical advantages. Preparation isn't busywork. It's strategy that pays off in combat and roleplay.

  • Living Environments

    Detailed economies, political tensions, and faction dynamics create emergent stories. NPCs have goals and act on them whether players are watching or not. The world moves.

  • Professional Adventurers

    Characters aren't chosen ones fulfilling prophecy. They're skilled specialists doing dangerous work. Competence matters. Training matters. The Guild doesn't send amateurs.

Built For You If...

This content is designed for DMs who:

  • Have full-time jobs outside of gaming
  • Prep in stolen moments, not dedicated hours
  • Want depth without density
  • Prefer logical worlds over pure fantasy
  • Like morally complex situations
  • Value player agency and consequences
  • Run games for other busy adults
  • Appreciate professional-grade organization

The Shattered Veil

Our flagship product is The Shattered Veil, a campaign setting where planar catastrophe transformed the world. One thousand years ago, an empress tried to control seventeen planar boundaries at once. She failed. Now fragments of other planes bleed permanently into reality, creating zones where alignment is geography and survival requires adaptation.

Each fragment zone is a complete campaign region with its own rules, societies, and conflicts. The Frozen Fjords is finished: 120,000+ words of content including four settlements, fifty NPCs, complete mechanics, and an introductory adventure. More zones are in development.
