Whisker & Wire

A solarpunk world where small animals build a better civilization from the ruins of ours. Humanity is gone. They consumed themselves out of greed, stripped the soil, heated the oceans, and choked the air. The green came back. And with it, something new.

A world being built. One story at a time.
