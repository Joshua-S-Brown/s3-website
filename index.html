Whisker & Wire

Humanity is gone. They consumed themselves out of greed, stripped the soil, heated the oceans, and choked the air. The green came back. And with it, something new. Small animals, intelligent and resourceful, building a better civilization from the ruins of ours.

Whisker & Wire is that world. It comes to life through short fiction, original art, and a tabletop roleplaying game, all growing in real time.

A world being built. One story at a time.

About Second Self Studios

The name is the mission. Every product we make is designed to give you a second self. A perspective you didn't have before. A way of seeing that wasn't available to you until you played that character, read that story, or looked at that image. What you do with that perspective when you come back to your own life is yours. But you won't see things exactly the same way you did before.
