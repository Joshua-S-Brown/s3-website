Story in 60 Seconds

The Frostfang Pack has guarded the eastern mountains for generations. Two weeks ago, a frost troll invaded their territory and claimed their den. The wolves have been investigating the kill sites, trying to warn travelers away. Merchants see wolves near corpses and assume causation.

The question becomes whether the party figures this out before killing the very creatures they should be saving.
